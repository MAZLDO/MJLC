Our Story

The Muslim Jewish Learning Collaboration (MJLC) was established in 2010 with the goal of building bridges between the Muslim and Jewish communities in the UK through education, dialogue, and collaborative social action.


We believe that by learning about each other's traditions, values, and practices in an atmosphere of mutual respect, we can break down stereotypes, build trust, and develop lasting friendships.


Our activities include text study sessions, social events, community projects, and public discussions on issues of common concern.

Our Projects

Scriptural Reasoning

Regular sessions where participants explore texts from both the Quran and Jewish scriptures, discovering shared values and different perspectives.

Community Dinners

Shared meals where members of both communities come together to build relationships and celebrate each other's traditions and holidays.

Social Action

Collaborative projects addressing food poverty, homelessness, and other social issues that affect our wider community.
